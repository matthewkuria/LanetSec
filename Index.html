The Departments

The following are the departments in Lanet Secondary School.

This department is led by Mr.Duncan Ndung'u with the help of Mr.Richard Kamau

The School administers CAT’s, contests, teachers’ own tests, RAT’s and examinations internally set for students throughout their four year course and the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education administered at the end of the course by the Kenya National Examination Council. The School also exposes it’s learners to joint examinations with various schools of the like minds within the Nakuru County and beyond that are used as a strategy to improve the learners’ performance. The School has always performed averagely over the years with the mean score oscillating between 4.1 and 5.2. With the strategic plan the trend is bound to change greatly.

The number of students joining university has always been rising with 2019 posting the highest number of students joining university. The trend should be improved through the strategies suggested in this strategic plan. Grade A plain and A- have been elusive throughout therefore, there is need for sustained effort for the school to realize the much elusive quality grades The school mean has for the last several years been oscillating between D+ and C.

    The world of today is undergoing a brisk transformation in terms of how communication is achieved.ICT(Information and Communication) is the integration of computers and telecommunication equipment for the purpose of communication and dissemination of information.

    Our School,Lanet Secondary school offers computer studies form form one to form four as an examinable subject aimed to equip learners with the kills to utilise ICT in its various forms to study,learn and conduct research for better understanding and improvement of performance.

    Computers are not only used for learning,teaching,exam analysis,fees management and admission of students into our school;they are also used for research,keeping records,exmaination(KCSE)registration and practicals and symposiums presentations in our school.

    Learners who are exposed to and trained on the competence of digitaln literacy are as much better and are able to learn digitally using computers,tablets,smartphones and internet.This enables them to be connected and competitive enough and to easily collaborate academically with other learners from all over the world.

    It is for this reason that our school has endeavored to offer computer studies,ICT and digital literacy skills to our learners.
