Our Student Leadership

The above are the top student leaders for the Student Council 2022/2023.

Our School has a unique system of leadership characterized mainly by student leadership. From morning to evening , the running of the School outside the classrooms is mainly by the school prefects who have for ages proved to be very effective in their mode of leadership. In the Kitchen, all the proceedings are controlled by the prefects themselves. They serve one another in turns and incase one of them is not in for the meal his food is preserved. The cleanliness of the compounds and even of the boys and girls themselves is checked and corrected by the prefects. A challenge to them is that they must meet the conditions before they can check the fellow boys and girls. This is normally not a problem to them. The Lanet prefectorial force is hierarchical, from the Sub Prefect to the School Governor. In between them lie the House and departmental prefects, Senior prefects and Captains. All these are answerable to those in higher authority and should give them their due respect.

Our clubs

Scout Movement of LSS

It's a member of International scouts Movement which is involved in various activities both within school and outside.The activities are: Flag raising Parades,Organizing venues for meetings,Community Service activities & attending scout meetings in various level e.g Founder's day.It's essential for instilling discipline and a sense of commitment and duty in the youths for them to become dependable citizens in future.

Patron is Mr Njiiri.

Mathematics Club

Maths Club activities include:Problem solving,making mathematics models,preparing projects,organising contests amd trips for the members.The objectives of the club is to cultivate a positive attitude towards the subject and also make the subject fun and esay to learn.

Matrons are Madam Gitau and Phyllis

Kenya Red Cross Society-LSS

It's a humanitarian relief organisation.The society aims at mobilising the power of humanity in raising funds for emergency operations and disaster management programmes for Nakuru County and Beyond.The Society engages in the following activities:(a)KRCS Humanity power walk annually and (b)Visiting Children's Homes and retirement Homes

Matron is Madam Mureithi.

Computer club

Lanet Computer Club is a club involved in training students about computers.In this Computer students are given tasks to complete and then they are shown on how to go about the task where they need help.Computer maintenance is also taught during the club meeting which really improves computer literacy leveles among students.
