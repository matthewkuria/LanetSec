The School Management







Our Principal

Madam Anne Awino is the Principal Lanet Secondary School

The Principal's Message

"We are looking forward to a productive partnership with you to ensure our children can achieve their highest potential. We recognize that to be successful in school, our children need support from both home and school. We know a strong partnership with you will make a significant difference in your child's education."

Lanet Secondary is a public school which prides as one of the best performing schools in Nakuru County. This makes students from most parts of the country who reside in Nakuru East Sub County choose to learn here. The school has continually performed well in both national examinations and co-curricular activities. With the strategic plan in place, we shall now focus on our targets in all sectors

The School takes great pride in the quality of education that is provided to its students. I note with self-confidence that the Board of Management, the Sponsor, Parents Association, Teachers, Non-teaching staff and community members have high expectation in helping to prepare our students for the future world endeavors.

Ann A.A Awino (Mrs.) Secretary to the B.O.M and P.A., Principal Lanet Secondary

Exams Officer

The School administers CAT’s, contests, teachers’ own tests, RAT’s and examinations internally set for students throughout their four year course and the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education administered at the end of the course by the Kenya National Examination Council. The School also exposes it’s learners to joint examinations with various schools of the like minds within the Nakuru County and beyond that are used as a strategy to improve the learners’ performance. The School has always performed averagely over the years with the mean score oscillating between 4.1 and 5.2. With the strategic plan the trend is bound to change greatly.
