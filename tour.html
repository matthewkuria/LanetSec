Did you know that?

Lanet Secondary school is a Sub County mixed day school. The school population has been growing steadily over the years having started as a single stream school to the current status with a student population of 1534 learners. In academic performance the school is one of the best public mixed day schools in Nakuru East Sub Country. In 2020 KCSE Exam, 12 of the candidates were admitted to the university. The school infrastructure has continued to improve. Modern facilities have been put in place to cater for the growing population. Currently the school has 22 Classrooms . There are 3 Science laboratories each having a capacity of 60 students as well as a Home Science lab with a capacity of 45 students. There is need for a bigger and more appropriate Home Science room. There is a Computer laboratory with a Capacity of 50 Students for computer studies. The school has 1 library, 1 Geography room, 1 French room, 1 Kitchen (Semi permanent). There are 3 water sources – Rain water, Kenya Military Academy piped water and water bowsers. There are 11 boys’ toilets with one Special need toilet, which serves the boys at a ratio 30:1 . Girls Toilets are 13, at a ratio of 25:1.
